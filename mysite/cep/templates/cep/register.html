About US

Since its inception; The IET UET Chapter has been providing an opportunity for maturing engineers to strut their stuff. Born in 2002, with the motto of promoting technology, scientific thinking and innovation, IET UET Chapter today is a body working for the betterment of professional abilities of students under the patronage of Sir Omar Imtiaz.
